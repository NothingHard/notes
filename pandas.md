### Math Method in Series
1. S.count(): number of elements
2. S.sum()  : summation of elements (numeric)
3. S.mean() : mean
4. S.std()  : standard deviation
5. S.min()  : minimum
6. S.max()  : maximum
7. S.median()  : median
8. S.mode() : most frequent element
9. S.describe(): count,mean,std,min,25%,50%,75%,max
